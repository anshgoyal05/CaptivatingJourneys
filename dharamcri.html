Budget-Friendly Escapes

For sports and nature lovers, the Dharamshala Cricket Stadium in Himachal Pradesh, India, is a unique destination. Known as one of the highest cricket stadiums in the world, it offers stunning views of the snow-clad Dhauladhar ranges while you enjoy the peaceful environment. My trip to Dharamshala was both scenic and affordable. With bus travel from Delhi costing around ₹1,200, a mid-range hotel stay at ₹1,500 per night, and entry to the stadium for a nominal fee, the entire weekend getaway cost under ₹5,000 ($60). Money-Saving Tips

  • Timing is Key: Travel during shoulder seasons for lower prices and fewer crowds.
  • Local Eats: Skip expensive restaurants and savor authentic cuisine at local markets or dhabas.
  • Free Attractions: Many cities offer free walking tours, temples, and scenic viewpoints.
